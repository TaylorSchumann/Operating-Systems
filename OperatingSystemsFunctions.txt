One operating system function would be turning on your phone. That is really step one for your operating systems. To turn on your system you need to hit the power button, which is normally located on your system unit, side or top of phone or for a laptop on the inside above the keyboard.
File manager is a great function. This function keeps track of your information, also known as the "file system." It deals with renaming, moving, copying or deleting. 
A third funtion is managing memory. With the use of this function is it to enhance the devices internal memory. Your system manages data to an area where it wishes it stored. It then will watch after that memory and release it when needed.
